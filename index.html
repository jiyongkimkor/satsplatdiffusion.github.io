Method

1

Photogrammetric Initialization

Stereo rays from satellite views converge on the surface, providing a geometric starting point.

2

Geometric Optimization

Monocular depth and multi-scale supervision turn the sparse, gappy surface into a dense, well-regularized one.

3

Shadow-Guided Generative Refinement

A geometrically calculated shadow map conditions diffusion-based refinement, sharpening textures while preserving structure.
