Method

1

Photogrammetric Initialization

Initializes 2D Gaussians from a DSM generated through classical stereo matching, establishing a solid geometric foundation.

2

Geometric Optimization

Monocular depth supervision, multi-scale refinement, and densification recover building facades that satellite views alone leave under-supervised.

3

Shadow-Guided Generative Refinement

Geometrically calculated shadow maps condition diffusion-based refinement, sharpening textures while keeping the geometry intact.
